About

Rooted in nature.
Led by the heart.

Suman Gidwani is a certified Yoga Educator with 200 & 300 RYT credentials, based in Hyderabad. With a deep love for nature 🦋 and a heart full of intention, she guides students through transformative practices that heal the body and calm the mind.

Her offerings span a wide range — from the strength and grace of Aerial Yoga to the gentle, therapeutic touch of Medical and Restorative Yoga. She also specialises in Pre & Post-Natal Yoga and Sound Healing, creating safe, nurturing spaces for all stages of life.
